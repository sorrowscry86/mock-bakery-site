A Passion for Pastries

Cathy's Cupcakes started in a small kitchen with a big dream: to bring joy to the Fort Smith community one cupcake at a time. Cathy, a third-generation baker, uses family recipes passed down through the years, adding her own modern twist. We believe in quality, flavor, and a little bit of magic in every bite.

Every morning, we bake from scratch using locally-sourced ingredients whenever possible. It's not just a business for us; it's our passion.
